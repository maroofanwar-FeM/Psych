# Feedback Loop — CoachConnect

A feedback loop is how CoachConnect improves over time: send a message, see how it lands, adjust, and repeat.

## In Practice

1. **Send** — automated message goes out to your 9 school groups.
2. **Observe** — check responses, engagement, or silence.
3. **Adjust** — update the message, timing, or template.
4. **Repeat** — each round gets better than the last.

Start simple in Step 2 (Make.com + Google Sheet), then refine as you build toward Step 5.
