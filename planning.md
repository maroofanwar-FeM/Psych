# CoachConnect — Simple Plan

> A tool that sends the right WhatsApp message to the right school group at the right time — automatically.
>
> **For:** Maroof Anwar, CPD Coach (9 schools)
> **Created:** 2026-06-10

---

## The Idea in One Line

Set up your messages and timings once. CoachConnect sends them to your 9 school groups for you — reminders, motivation, follow-ups — so you don't have to.

---

## One Thing to Know First

WhatsApp doesn't officially allow auto-sending to **groups**. There's a workaround that *does* send to groups, but it can get a number **banned** if used carelessly.

**So the golden rule: use a spare phone number, never your personal one.**

That's it. The plan below is built around staying safe.

---

## The Plan: 5 Simple Steps

### Step 1 — Get Ready
- Get a **cheap second SIM** = your "CoachConnect number."
- Add that number to all 9 school groups.
- Write down your plan in plain words, e.g.:
  - *Monday 8am* → motivation
  - *Day before a session* → reminder
  - *Day after a session* → thank-you / follow-up
- Write a few ready-to-use messages for each type.

**You'll have:** your number in all groups + a simple list of "what to send when."

---

### Step 2 — Try It Cheaply (No Coding)
- Sign up for **Make.com** (free, click-to-build, no code).
- Put your messages in a **Google Sheet** (group, message, day, time).
- Make.com reads the sheet and sends each message at the right time, to the right group.
- Run it for a few weeks on your real groups and see how it goes.

**You'll have:** real automated messages going out — controlled from a simple spreadsheet. **This proves the idea works before you spend money building anything.**

---

### Step 3 — Build the Real App (You Only)
This turns the spreadsheet setup into a proper website you log into.

> ⚠️ This step needs a developer. Either **hire one**, **partner with a techie coach**, or have an **AI assistant build it**. Be honest about which.

The app will let you:
- **Log in.**
- **Connect WhatsApp** (scan a QR code, like WhatsApp Web).
- **Add your 9 schools** and their groups.
- **Save your messages** as reusable templates.
- **Set your schedule** ("Every Monday 8am, send motivation to all groups").
- **See what was sent** and whether WhatsApp is still connected.

**You'll have:** a real CoachConnect website that runs your whole plan automatically.

---

### Step 4 — Add Helpful Extras
Two simple, useful additions once the app works:

1. **AI message helper** — a button that writes a friendly message draft for you. You type a short idea ("reminder for tomorrow's session"), it writes a warm version, you edit if needed, then save or send. *It just helps you write faster — you stay in control.*

2. **Send Now button** — for urgent news, send one message to all groups instantly with one click.

**You'll have:** faster message writing + instant broadcasts when you need them.

---

### Step 5 — Share With Other Coaches (Later)
Once it works great for you, open it up so **other NIETE coaches** can sign up and run their own schools and messages — each in their own private account.

> This is the biggest step. Get **NIETE's support** first, since it involves more coaches, more cost, and teacher data.

**You'll have:** a platform any NIETE coach can join and use.

---

## Suggested Order

1. Do **Step 1 + Step 2 first** — cheap, fast, proves the idea.
2. **Show the results to NIETE** to get their support.
3. Build **Steps 3 & 4** as your own daily tool.
4. Only then tackle **Step 5** (sharing with all coaches).

---

## Quick Safety Checklist

- [ ] Use a **spare number**, never your personal WhatsApp.
- [ ] Don't blast all groups at the exact same second — small gaps look natural.
- [ ] Keep messages **warm and human**, not robotic.
- [ ] Get **NIETE's approval** early.
- [ ] Have a **manual backup** in case the number ever gets blocked.
